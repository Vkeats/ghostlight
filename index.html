founder

Vincent Keats

Software engineer and actor based in Vancouver.

Ghostlight started in 2024 while working as a dispatcher at IATSE Local 118. The Board was built to automate the job, cutting dispatch time from 45 minutes to 5. What began as a personal tool now manages 3,400+ workers across hundreds of shows. The Guild followed, bringing the same thinking to casting and creative teams.

Studied theatre at CCPA, then learned to code through Harvard's CS50 and #100Devs while dispatching full-time. Still performs in the same theatres the software supports.
